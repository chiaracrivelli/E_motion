[E·motion] it’s a Typeface designed for a film or small clip subtitles.
[E·motion] have been conceived not only to translate the dialogue, in fact he rebuilds the emotion and sound effect of the different scenes you see on videos.
The font presents nine variables emotion with also a neutral weight one. The choice of this nine variable emotions, has been based on the studies of the emotions by Robert Plutchik and Paul Elkman, who has found the eight primal emotions. Based on these researches, emotions are made visible by our face body language.
